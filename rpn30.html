RPN 30

Reverse Polish Notation ("RPN") is an alternative way to complete calculations. Many find it to be more intuitive and user-friendly than the "normal" way of using a calculator. RPN works entering numbers first and then the operator (i.e. 53 47 + will give you 53 + 47 = 100). RPN 30 is my artistic interpretation of the ideal form for a calculator, taking inspiration from the standard Apple iPhone calculator. No ads, no paid features, always free to use.

Download link: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/rpn-30/id1451413517
